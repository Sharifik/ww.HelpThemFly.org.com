About Our Foundation

Help Them Fly Foundation Uganda is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving the lives of underprivileged communities across Africa. Founded in 2015, we have been working tirelessly to provide essential needs to those who need them most.

Our mission is to create sustainable solutions that empower communities to break the cycle of poverty. We believe that access to clean water, quality education, and adequate food are fundamental human rights that everyone deserves.

With your support, we've impacted over 50,000 lives across 20 communities in Uganda, providing clean water sources, educational materials, and food supplies to families in need.
